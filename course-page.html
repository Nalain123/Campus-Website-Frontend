BBA (Hons)

Those who have completed FA/ F.Sc / I.Com/ A-Level or equivalent with at least 45% marks are eligible for admission. Age: A candidate must not be more than 22 years of age on 1st October of the year of the admission, provided that the Vice chancellor may relax age limit in a very exceptional cases. Requirements of the degree The degree is awarded after completion of 136 credit hours with minimum CGPA of 2.50 and an internship of 6 to 8 weeks with any organization. The degree requirements are normally completed in 4 years spreading over 8 semesters.
